The Fastest Way to Split PDF Files Online

Need to extract a single page from a large document? Or maybe you want to separate a PDF into individual files for every page? The MyTools PDF Splitter makes it effortless. Unlike other tools that require you to upload sensitive documents to a server, our tool processes your files locally in your browser using advanced JavaScript technology.

Why use our PDF Splitter?

Custom Page Ranges

Precision control. Type exactly what you need (e.g., "1-5, 8, 11-13") and we'll create a new PDF with just those pages.

Burst Mode (Extract All)

Turn a 100-page document into 100 separate PDF files in one click. We automatically ZIP them for easy downloading.

100% Private & Secure

Your documents never leave your device. Since we don't upload your files to a cloud server, your data remains completely confidential.

No Limits

Split as many files as you want. There are no daily limits, watermarks, or hidden costs.

How to Extract Pages from a PDF?

Follow these simple steps to separate your PDF pages:

  1. Upload PDF: Click the box above to select the PDF file you want to split.
  2. Select Mode: Choose "Custom Range" to pick specific pages, or "Extract All" to separate every page.
  3. Enter Pages: If using Custom Range, type the page numbers (e.g., 1, 3-5).
  4. Download: Click the button to instantly download your new PDF or ZIP file.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is it safe to split confidential documents?

Yes. Because our tool runs client-side, your file is never sent over the internet. It is processed directly by your computer's processor.

Can I merge the selected pages?

Absolutely. By default, the "Custom Range" mode merges your selected pages into a single new PDF file. If you uncheck the merge option, you will get separate files.

Does this work on Mac and Windows?

Yes! MyTools works in any modern web browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge, Firefox) on Windows, Mac, Linux, and even mobile devices.
