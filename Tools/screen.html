Free Online Screen Recorder

Capture your desktop, a specific window, or a browser tab without installing heavy software. The MyTools Screen Recorder is perfect for creating tutorials, recording gameplay, or saving video calls.

It utilizes the browser's getDisplayMedia API to capture high-quality video directly from your screen.

How to Record Your Screen?

  • Start: Click "Start Recording". Your browser will ask which screen or window to share.
  • Record: Perform your task. The tool is recording in the background.
  • Stop: Click "Stop Recording" (or the "Stop Sharing" button in your browser).
  • Download: Preview the video and click "Download Video" to save it as a WebM file.

Key Benefits

No Watermarks

Record clean, professional video without annoying branding overlaid on your content.

Selectable Area

Choose to record your entire monitor, just one app window, or a single Chrome tab.

Privacy Guaranteed

The video is rendered in your browser. We do not (and cannot) view your screen recordings.

Instant Save

No rendering wait times. The video is available for download the second you stop recording.
