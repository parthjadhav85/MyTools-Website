The Easiest Way to Cut Audio Online

Editing audio shouldn't require expensive software like Audacity or Adobe Audition. With the MyTools Audio Cutter, you can trim songs, create iPhone ringtones, or remove awkward silence from recordings directly in your browser. We use a professional visual waveform editor so you can see exactly where to cut.

Why use our MP3 Cutter?

Visual Precision

Don't guess the timestamps. Our tool generates a visual waveform of your audio. Zoom in to find the exact millisecond where a beat starts or ends.

100% Private

Your audio files are processed locally using WebAssembly. We never upload your songs or recordings to a server, ensuring total privacy.

All Formats Supported

Whether you have a standard MP3, a high-quality WAV, or an OGG file, our tool handles it seamlessly.

Make Ringtones

Select the chorus of your favorite song, trim it down to 30 seconds, and download it to set as your mobile ringtone.

How to Trim Audio Files?

  1. Upload Audio: Click the box to select your MP3, WAV, or OGG file.
  2. Select Region: Drag the handles on the waveform to highlight the part you want to keep.
  3. Fine Tune: Use the "Zoom" and "Play" buttons to ensure your selection is perfect.
  4. Trim & Save: Click "Trim & Download" to instantly save your new audio clip.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Does this reduce audio quality?

No. We use advanced audio processing to ensure your trimmed file retains the highest possible quality from the original source.

Can I use this on my phone?

Yes! The tool is fully responsive and works great on iPhone and Android browsers for editing voice memos or songs on the go.

Is there a file size limit?

Since we process files locally on your device, there is no strict server limit. You can edit large podcasts or long music mixes easily.
