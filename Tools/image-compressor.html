The Best Free Online Image Compressor

Is your website loading slowly? Do you need to email a photo but the file is too large? The MyTools Image Compressor solves this instantly. We use smart compression algorithms to significantly reduce the file size of your JPG, PNG, and WEBP images while keeping the visual quality perfectly clear.

Why use our Photo Optimizer?

Smart Compression

We balance file size and image quality. You can reduce a 5MB photo to under 500KB without noticing any blur or artifacts.

100% Private (No Uploads)

Unlike other sites, we don't upload your photos to a server. All compression happens locally in your browser. Your personal photos stay on your device.

Supports All Formats

Whether you have a standard JPEG from a camera, a PNG screenshot, or a modern WEBP file, our tool handles them all efficiently.

Custom Control

Use our slider to choose your exact compression level. You decide if you want maximum savings or maximum quality.

How to Compress Images Online?

  1. Upload: Click the box to select your image (JPG, PNG, or WEBP).
  2. Adjust: Use the "Quality" slider to choose how much you want to compress.
  3. Resize (Optional): If the image is too wide (e.g., 4000px), use the Width slider to shrink dimensions for even smaller file sizes.
  4. Download: Click "Apply & Compress" to see the savings, then download your optimized image.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Does this ruin image quality?

No. We use "Lossy" compression which removes data the human eye can't see. Your image will look virtually identical to the original, just much smaller.

Why is client-side better?

It's faster because you don't have to wait for uploads/downloads, and it's safer because your data never touches a cloud server.

What is the best setting for websites?

For websites, we recommend a Quality of 75% and a Max Width of 1920px. This usually results in crisp images under 200KB.
