Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Data Collection

Video Looper operates as a client-side application. This means that when you use our tool to loop videos, the processing happens entirely within your web browser. We do not upload your video files to any external server.

2. Local Storage

We may use local storage (cookies) to save your preferences, such as your last used loop count or theme settings, to improve your user experience.

3. Third-Party Services

If you use the YouTube feature, you are subject to YouTube's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. The video player is embedded directly from YouTube servers.

4. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us via the Contact page.
