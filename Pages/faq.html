Frequently Asked Questions

How do I loop a YouTube video?

Simply copy the URL of the YouTube video you want to watch, paste it into the "Paste YouTube URL" box on our home page, select the number of loops, and click Start.

Why is my direct video link not working?

Direct links must end in .mp4 or .webm. Also, some websites block their videos from being played on other sites (CORS policy). If a link fails, try downloading the video and using the "File Upload" option instead.

Is my data private?

Yes. Video processing happens entirely in your browser. We do not upload your files to any server.

Does this work on mobile?

Yes, but modern mobile browsers often block "Autoplay" to save data. You may need to manually tap "Play" after the video resets for the first time.
