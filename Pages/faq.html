Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MyTools.

General

Is MyTools completely free?
Yes! All tools on our platform, including the Video Downloader, PDF Merger, and Media Converter, are 100% free to use with no hidden subscription fees.
Do I need to install any software?
No. MyTools is a web-based platform. Tools like the Screen Recorder, Voice Recorder, and Video Looper run entirely in your browser. Advanced tools like the Media Converter run on our secure cloud server, so you don't need to install heavy software on your device.

Privacy & Security

Are my uploaded files safe?
Absolutely.

1. Browser Tools: Tools like Screen/Webcam Recorder and Video Looper process data locally on your device. Nothing is ever sent to our servers.
2. Server Tools: For tools like the PDF Merger and Converter, your files are uploaded to a temporary secure folder for processing and are automatically deleted immediately after the download is generated. We do not store your data.

YouTube Downloader

Why is there no audio on my downloaded video?
This usually happens if you select a high-resolution format (like 4K) that uses the AV1 codec, which some older players don't support.
The Fix: Our updated tool now forces H.264 (MP4) format, which works on all devices. If you still have issues, try using VLC Media Player.
What is the maximum video quality supported?
We support downloads up to 1080p Full HD with audio automatically merged. We also support extracting high-quality MP3 audio from videos.

Converters & PDF Tools

What file formats does the Media Converter support?
You can upload almost any video or audio format (WEBM, MKV, AVI, MOV). We can convert them into:
  • MP4: Standard video format.
  • MP3: Audio only.
  • WAV: High-quality uncompressed audio.
  • GIF: Animated images.
How many PDFs can I merge at once?
Currently, there is no hard limit! You can select as many PDF files as you want from your computer, and our tool will stitch them into a single document in the order you selected.
